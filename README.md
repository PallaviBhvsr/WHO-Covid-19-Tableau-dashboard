# Tableau Dashboard: COVID - 19 World Health Organizations (WHO)

## Overview: 
This Tableau Dashboard presents visual insights into the global trends of COVID-19 cases and fatalities, utilizing data sourced from the World Health 
Organization (WHO) spanning from January 5, 2020, to March 3, 2024. The dashboard aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of the pandemic's trajectory 
over the past few years and facilitate informed analysis for stakeholders.

## Key Features:

**Interactive Visualizations** : Explore trends in confirmed cases, Deaths, and other key metrics through dynamic and interactive visualizations.

**Data Cleansing Process**: The dataset underwent thorough cleaning and preparation using Microsoft Excel, ensuring data accuracy and integrity before importing it into Tableau for visualization.

**Global Perspective**: Gain insights into the global impact of COVID-19, with the ability to filter data by region, date range, and other relevant parameters.

**Data Sources**: The dashboard utilizes data provided by the World Health Organization (WHO), offering reliable and up-to-date information on the pandemic.

## How To use:

**Accessing the Dashboard**: Click [here](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/pallavi.bhavsar/viz/COVID-19DASHBOARDWORLDHEALTHORGANIZATIONWHO/DEATHSDASHBOARD) to access the Tableau Dashboard.

**Interacting with Visualizations**: Use filters and tooltips to interact with the visualizations and explore specific data points of interest.

**Navigating the Dashboard**: Navigate between different views and panels to gain insights into various aspects of COVID-19 trends globally.

**Feedback and Collaboration**: Your feedback is invaluable! Feel free to share your thoughts, suggestions, or insights derived from the dashboard. Let's collaborate in deciphering the pandemic's impact and charting a path forward.
